70s Music Trivia Quiz

Ten questions, fresh every round. From arena rock to the dance floor — how well do you know the music of the 70s?

Each round pulls 10 random questions from a pool of 50, spanning classic rock, disco, soul, funk, country, prog and punk.

Think You Know the 70s?

This 70s music quiz is built for genuine fans — the kind who can tell their prog from their punk and their Philly soul from their Southern rock. Ten questions per round, drawn fresh from a deep pool, so no two games feel the same. It's a fun, fast way to test how much of the decade really stuck.

Good 70s music trivia isn't about obscure gotchas; it's about celebrating the records, artists and moments that mattered. Expect questions on landmark albums, chart-topping singles, band line-ups and the milestones that shaped 70s rock music and beyond. Some you'll know instantly; others will jog a memory you forgot you had.

Whether you're a classic rock trivia veteran or just love a good music quiz, give it a spin, then challenge a friend to beat your score. The 70s would be proud.
